Tips to hit the maximum productivity
by saron on January 19, 2021, 0 comments

So you probably are sitting where ever you are and reading thsi article because you wandered how people make the best use of their time and hit that maximum productivity. Well you are at the right place so with out further due lets go over those tips...

‘Try to set goals on a daily basis’.

Take a note book out and just try to ask yourself what things you need to accomplish that day.Write them down on your notebook so that you can keep track of things you accomplished.But remember its better to accomplish small number of tasks than to just go here and there trying to touch every task with out accomplishing one.

‘Do the hardest thing first’

This i the one productivity tip that i have always used. Not only it helped me to use the best out of my time it also gives me the confidence and boost that energy and strength to do the rest of the tasks. Try to focus doing that task and only that. Keep your self out from distractions like phonecall, chat, emai and socialmedia.

‘Just do it’

when i wrote these tips i was int the middle of school break which means i had alot of days to do this. But as soon as the idea of writing strikes me i just brought a paper and pen and start writing. It didn't felt like the day to do so but i did it anyway. The best way to bit procrastination is by commiting your self to doing 5 minutes of whatever it is you got to do.Trust me you will end up finishing it just like i did!

‘Know your priorities’

I dont have time actually means its not on my priority list. Many things might look good or sound perfect to hear but are not right to be practiced.Embrace the reality and know what to prioritize.Do not load yourself with number of tasks. Instead identify your priorities and focus on them. The other things can wait.

‘Sleep ofcourse ’

Sleep is crucial for basically everything.A person once said "overworking hindera performances and lead to burnout". nexttime you are tempted to stay up late get some sleep and revisit the task in the morning instead. Taking the time to sleep may actually help your brain to recharge, boost your performance and so increase your work effectivenss.
