Books

As a student it is expected from me to read both educational and fictional books. Well frankly speaking reading educational books might be boring sometimes. so aside from watching football or dancing or cooking I some times read fictional books.

Not only I entertain my self while reading the book it also gives me new way of life and different perspective of it.It also tends to increase my creativity and imagination beacause i cast my self as one of the characters inside the book.

So If you are a person who is not fond of reading books then you might be missing out. People often don’t understand the Benefits of Reading Books, Some says it’s a waste of time, Some Find it boring, and for many such reasons people believe or can say have a mindset that reading books is not so useful and it’s of no use, just a time waste, energy waste, However Fact is opposite to such mindset, There are various reasons why Reading is very important and beneficial.
