3 things i call my hobbies
by saron on January 20, 2021, 0 comments

Even machines need rest to perform at their best for a long period of time and so does human beings.I personally call the followings as my hobbies since i enjoy doing them and they give me a little bit of calmness and rest to my exhausted mind.I also wrote my perspective towards each.

‘Football’.

They say that “football is like life - it requires perserverance, self-denial, hard work, sacrifice, dedication and respect for authority.” Well said!Some people think that foot ball is just a game where you roll the ball over the pitch. Wrongly taken! It is a game where the coach applies his philosopy on the players so that they deliver it well.It is like a teacher-student situation. The players also use their brain to deliver their coache's philosopy.Their legs are just tools. It is a game where players play with passion to entertain their fans.

‘Dancing’

Dancing is so much more than just grooving on the dance floor to your favorite tunes. In fact, you’ll be surprised how many benefits are associated with dancing. Not only does it train your brain and your body’s motor skills but it also is an excellent exercise for your entire body. As such, it helps you to stay physically and mentally fit. Even more so, dancing regularly can improve your general well-being, boosts your self-esteem and has also been shown to improve your social skills.
